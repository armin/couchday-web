Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The Short Version

Couch Day is designed with your privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device. We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand how the app is used, but we never see your personal information or data.

What We Collect

Day Plan Data

Your activities, tasks, and history are stored only on your iPhone using Apple's on-device storage. We never see this data. We can't access it. It's yours.

Analytics

We collect anonymous usage analytics to understand how the app is used so we can make it better.

We collect:

  • App version
  • Device type and iOS version
  • General usage patterns (e.g., which screens are viewed)
  • Crash reports

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal information
  • Your activity and task names or data
  • Your location
  • Any data that could identify you personally

Our analytics are privacy-first, anonymous by design, and cannot be used to track you across apps.

Data Storage

Your activity and task data is stored locally on your device. We never see it, and it never leaves your phone.

If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it.

Third-Party Services

We use a privacy-focused analytics service to collect anonymous usage data. We do not use advertising networks or sell your data to anyone.

Future Features

If we add features that involve additional data collection in the future (such as iCloud sync or AI-powered suggestions), we will:

  • Update this privacy policy
  • Clearly explain what data is involved
  • Make those features optional

Children's Privacy

Couch Day does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The analytics we collect are anonymous and cannot identify individual users of any age.

Your Choices

Our analytics are anonymous by design. If you prefer, you can limit device analytics in iOS Settings

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
