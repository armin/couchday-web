Frequently Asked Questions

What is Couchday?

Couchday is a gentle day planner for people who want structure without the stress. Plan your day like a playlist — add what feels right, skip what doesn't, and let the timeline handle the rest.

How is this different from other planner apps?

Most planners punish you when things don't go to plan. Couchday doesn't. Your timeline is alive — it auto-adjusts around fixed events and flows with your day. No rigid time blocks. No guilt when things shift.

Is it free?

Couchday is free to use with all core features included.

Does it sync across devices?

Yes. Couchday uses iCloud to seamlessly sync across iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Pick up exactly where you left off.

Does it work with my calendar?

Yes. Couchday integrates with Apple Calendar so your existing events flow naturally into your timeline — no double entry.

What are Live Activities?

Live Activities let you see your current activity on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island in real time — so you always know what's happening without opening the app.

Is there a widget?

Yes — Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets in multiple sizes. See your day at a glance without opening the app.

What happens if my day falls apart?

Nothing bad. Your timeline adjusts. Missed something? It's gone — no red marks, no shaming. Just carry on from where you are.

What is Liquid Glass?

Liquid Glass is Apple's latest design language, introduced in iOS 26. Couchday is built from the ground up with it — bringing stunning depth, translucency, and a polished feel to every screen.

Is it available on Android?

Couchday is exclusive to Apple platforms — iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
